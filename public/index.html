Why take the tour with us, and not with others?

City Night Views

Skyline vibes + the best rooftop energy in the city,. Take in breathtaking panoramic views as the city lights come alive at night. Enjoy exclusive rooftop locations where the atmosphere, music, and crowd create the perfect balance.

Salsa & Bachata Spots

Feel the rhythm — dance, learn, and vibe all night. Experience the heart of Latin culture in hand-picked salsa and bachata venues. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced dancer.

“Skip the wait — walk straight in like a VIP.” Complimentary shots included. Forget long lines and crowded entrances. Enjoy priority access to the best venues in the city with VIP treatment from the moment you arrive.

Meet Your Guide

¡HIGHLY RECOMMENDED!

Alejandro Martinez

local guide

Meet Your Guide I’m Alejandro, born and raised in Medellín, and passionate about showing the real energy of my city. I’ve spent years crafting unforgettable nightlife experiences for travellers who seek more than a basic tour—they crave authenticity, culture, and a truly memorable night out. I speak English, French, Italian, and Arabic, which helps me connect with people from all over the world and make everyone feel at home. With me, you’re not just joining a tour—you’re experiencing Medellín through the eyes of a local who truly loves this city. Get ready for the best night of your trip.
